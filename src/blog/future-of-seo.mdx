Search engine optimization (SEO) is evolving as AI becomes more integrated into search algorithms. Understanding these changes is crucial for brands that want to maintain visibility and relevance.

## Smarter Search Algorithms

AI-powered search engines can better understand user intent, making keyword stuffing obsolete and rewarding high-quality, relevant content.

## Voice Search and Natural Language

With the rise of voice assistants, optimizing for natural language queries is more important than ever. AI helps search engines interpret conversational queries, changing the SEO landscape for good.
